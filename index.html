I believe great design is invisible — it guides without demanding attention.

Hey, I'm Saka Akeem Ayopelumi (Harqheem) — a product designer from Nigeria, passionate about crafting digital experiences that feel effortless and meaningful. I've worked across industries, from AI tools to transport apps, taking products from the first sketch all the way to polished, shippable interfaces.

I care deeply about clean visuals, smart information architecture, and making sure every user feels considered. Design, to me, is a conversation between function and feeling.

Outside work? I'm gaming, reading, watching football, or getting embarrassed on the lawn tennis court 🎾.

My Process

How I

work.

DESIGN

"Good design is about understanding the problem so deeply that the solution becomes obvious."

01
Discover & Listen

I start by deeply understanding the problem space — user interviews, competitive analysis, and stakeholder conversations. No assumptions, only evidence.

02
Define the Problem

Synthesizing research into clear problem statements, user personas, and opportunity maps. Defining the right problem before designing anything.

03
Design & Prototype

From rough sketches to polished high-fidelity prototypes. Rapid iteration, design reviews, and close collaboration with engineers throughout.

04
Test & Refine

Usability testing, A/B experiments, and data review to validate decisions. I treat shipping as the beginning of the design process, not the end.
